Captured Love

Love is captured forever from a camera lense

Posted by Jenna Buell in Fall 2023

No More a Stranger

This one isn’t a stranger. But she was… Only like a month or so before this photo was taken. I guess that’s pretty cool.

She impulse buys concert tickets. She laughs at my jokes. She asks me for advice on how to pose for a picture.

But a couple months ago… She wasn’t anyone to me. How beautiful is that?? Someone is always something great, even if you haven’t recognized it yet. Remember that the next time you walk past people on the street that you’ll never stop and meet. We are surrounded by wonderful people all the time.

An Embrace

They ran across the plaza to embrace each other. It made me wonder when the last time they saw each other was. I said a prayer these reunions would happen more often for them.

Nafplio, Greece 10 . 18 . 2023

Petra

Couples wandering in Petra, Jordan. A place of one of the Seven Wonders of the World, and it had me wondering about love. Love is shown in these two photos. But there’s more kinds of love than couples. Every person in these two photos is loved by someone. Whether they are walking with them or not. And I think that’s important to remember. Everyone is someone’s dear friend. Everyone is someone’s family member. Everyone is someone to God. My favorite person in these photos is the woman in the bottom-left corner of the second photo of the younger couple. She’s smiling. At who? I don’t know. Someone out of frame. But I pray for more experiences in her life that leave her smile gentle and eyes full of love. God bless them all, whether they are the focus of the photo or someone in the background love is grander than we imagine.
